Queens Community Board 10

BoroughQueens
BoroCD410

Like everything in the city, there's a lot going on here. QN CB10 had a median household income of $92,520 in 2023, ranking 19th among all 59 community board districts citywide. Queens is the most economically diverse borough in the city, ranging from high-income districts to neighborhoods with significant poverty and rent burden. The poverty rate was 11.4%, below the 18.2% citywide figure. Median gross rent was $2,100 in 2023 — 15th most expensive in the city.
