Service landscape

CB6 includes major hospital access near Park Slope, neighborhood clinics and physician offices, independent and chain pharmacies, and social-service providers supporting immigrants, older adults, and low-income households across Carroll Gardens, Cobble Hill, Gowanus, Park Slope, Red Hook, and surrounding blocks.

How to use this page

Search by provider name, street, or service type. Use the category filter to narrow the directory and map. Selecting a card zooms directly to that site on the map for faster neighborhood review on mobile and desktop.

Data source: locally saved BKCB6 healthcare dataset assembled from OpenStreetMap facility records within the CB6 service area on 2026-04-20. Social-service coverage reflects mapped providers and is not a complete inventory of all programs.

Coverage notes

Hospitals are relatively few, while pharmacies, clinics, and office-based care are distributed across multiple neighborhood corridors.

The directory below is intended as a practical neighborhood map rather than a licensing database. It is most useful for finding nearby care types, browsing providers by category, and checking whether a service cluster exists near a specific corridor such as 5th Avenue, Smith Street, Atlantic Avenue, or Red Hook.
