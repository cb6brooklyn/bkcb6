District Overview — Furman Center
Demographics

In 2023, Clinton/Chelsea was the city’s 44th largest neighborhood by population out of 59 neighborhoods. Among the 59 districts, it has the 45th largest proportion of nonwhite population, the 8th highest median income, and the 7th most expensive rents. From 2010 to 2024, the district added 19,232 new housing units, 13,457 units of which were market rate and 5,550 units of which were income restricted.
