## Description
CB6 & Beyond is the civic dashboard for Brooklyn Community Board 6 and beyond.

Who gets what, when, and how. When it all boils down, that is what policy is. CB6 & Beyond puts the data behind those decisions in your pocket.

Features:
- Interactive maps: land use, zoning, landmarks, Gowanus rezoning, transportation, parks, public safety
- Transportation: bike routes, bus stops, subway lines, Citi Bike stations, Sammy's Law school zones
- Land use by lot: all 13,746 CB6 parcels with zoning, ownership, and year built
- Public safety: NYPD precincts and sectors, FDNY battalions and firehouses, DSNY districts
- Budget: NYC FY2027 and NYS budget process explained
- Government org charts: NYC and NY State
- Rental rankings: Brooklyn and citywide rent maps by community board
- Elections and legislative districts
- CB6 calendar, permits, 311 complaints, liquor licenses
- Gowanus rezoning: all 3,094 lots with zone, land use, NYCHA, and M/R districts
- Brooklyn Marine Terminal
- Beyond CB6: all 59 NYC community boards
