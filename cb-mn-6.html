Stuyvesant Town/Turtle Bay (MN06) Profile

View full profile ↗

Summary

In 2023, Stuyvesant Town/Turtle Bay was the city’s 4th largest neighborhood by population out of 59 neighborhoods. It has the 54th largest proportion of nonwhite population, the 7th highest median income, and the 3rd most expensive rents of the city’s 59 neighborhoods. From 2010 to 2024, the neighborhood added 5,236 new housing units, 4,754 units of which were market rate and 482 units of which were income restricted.

  • In 2023, Stuyvesant Town/Turtle Bay was the city’s 4th largest neighborhood by population out of 59 neighborhoods. It has the 54th largest proportion of nonwhite population, the 7th highest median income, and the 3rd most expensive rents of the city’s 59 neighborhoods.
  • Median household income in 2023 was $149,640, about 88% more than citywide median household income ($79,480). The poverty rate in Stuyvesant Town/Turtle Bay was 10.0% in 2023 compared to 18.2% citywide.
  • As of 2023, the change in median gross rent outpaced the change in median household income by 32.8 percentage points.
  • In 2023, the homeownership rate in Stuyvesant Town/Turtle Bay was 31.5%, which is lower than the citywide share of 32.5%.
  • The serious crime rate, including property and violent crime types, was 8.9 serious crimes per 1,000 residents in 2024, compared to 13.6 serious crimes per 1,000 residents citywide.
