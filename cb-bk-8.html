District Overview — Furman Center
Demographics

BK CB8 ranks 54th in population among NYC’s 59 community board districts. Among the 59 districts, it has the 31st largest proportion of nonwhite population, the 18th highest median income, and the 13th most expensive rents. From 2010 to 2024, the district added 5,751 new housing units, 4,016 units of which were market rate and 1,735 units of which were income restricted.
