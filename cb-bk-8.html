Summary

In 2023, Crown Heights/Prospect Heights was the city’s 54th largest neighborhood by population out of 59 neighborhoods. It has the 31st largest proportion of nonwhite population, the 18th highest median income, and the 13th most expensive rents of the city’s 59 neighborhoods. From 2010 to 2024, the neighborhood added 5,751 new housing units, 4,016 units of which were market rate and 1,735 units of which were income restricted.
