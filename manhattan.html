Furman Center — Manhattan Borough Profile

View full profile ↗
Overview

Manhattan is the city’s 3rd largest borough by population. It has the 4th largest proportion of nonwhite population, the highest median income, and the most expensive rents of the city’s 5 boroughs. Between 2010 and 2024, the borough added 66,580 new housing units—49,113 market rate, 17,228 income restricted.
