Like everything in the city, there's a lot going on here. BX CB2 ranks 57th citywide for median income. The Bronx has the lowest median income of any borough — four of the five lowest-income community districts in the city are here.

This is the district page for Bronx Community Board 2. The district covers Hunts Point, Longwood, and Morrisania.

If you have thoughts on where those neighborhood lines should actually be drawn — and plenty of people do — feel free to reach out at Mike@bkcb6.org. Lots of different opinions on this and always happy to hear them.
