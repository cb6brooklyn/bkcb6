Queens Community Board 4

BoroughQueens
BoroCD404

Like everything in the city, there's a lot going on here. QN CB4 had a median household income of $73,470 in 2023, ranking 34th among all 59 community board districts citywide. Queens is the most economically diverse borough in the city, ranging from high-income districts to neighborhoods with significant poverty and rent burden. The poverty rate was 17.3%, below the 18.2% citywide figure. Median gross rent was $1,960 in 2023 — 22nd most expensive in the city. 31.1% of renter households were severely rent burdened.
