District Overview — Furman Center
Demographics

BK CB14 ranks 24th in population among NYC’s 59 community board districts. Among the 59 districts, it has the 38th largest proportion of nonwhite population, the 25th highest median income, and the 33rd most expensive rents. From 2010 to 2024, the district added 4,359 new housing units, 3,460 units of which were market rate and 899 units of which were income restricted.
