NYC Election Maps & Charts

This standalone page pulls together the citywide community-board winner-margin maps and the borough chart images already published across the site. It is designed as a simpler, mobile-friendly place to review the mayoral result and the three housing-related ballot proposals without the extra navigation around them.

Just the maps and charts

The four interactive maps below show the citywide result by community board for the 2025 mayoral race and Ballot Questions 2, 3, and 4. Under that, the page groups the existing borough chart images for the same four topics so you can compare borough patterns quickly.

The legend wording is intentionally simplified for first-time viewers. Instead of repeating a raw endpoint like 68.4% at the edge of the bar, the shared legend now explains the scale as a rounded range.
