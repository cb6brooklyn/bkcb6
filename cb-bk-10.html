District Overview — Furman Center
Demographics

In 2023, Bay Ridge/Dyker Heights was the city’s 39th largest neighborhood by population out of 59 neighborhoods. Among the 59 districts, it has the 47th largest proportion of nonwhite population, the 20th highest median income, and the 30th most expensive rents. From 2010 to 2024, the district added 431 new housing units, 426 units of which were market rate and 5 units of which were income restricted.
