This is the district page for Brooklyn Community Board 10. The district covers Bay Ridge · Dyker Heights.

If you have thoughts on where those neighborhood lines should actually be drawn — and plenty of people do — feel free to reach out at Mike@bkcb6.org. Lots of different opinions on this and always happy to hear them.

Like everything in the city, there's a lot going on here. BK CB10 ranks 20th citywide for median income. Brooklyn spans from some of the wealthiest community districts in New York City to neighborhoods with poverty rates over 30%.

District Overview — Furman Center
Demographics

In 2023, Bay Ridge/Dyker Heights was the city’s 39th largest neighborhood by population out of 59 neighborhoods. Among the 59 districts, it has the 47th largest proportion of nonwhite population, the 20th highest median income, and the 30th most expensive rents. From 2010 to 2024, the district added 431 new housing units, 426 units of which were market rate and 5 units of which were income restricted.
