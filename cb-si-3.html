This is the district page for Staten Island Community Board 3. The district covers Tottenville · Great Kills · Annadale · Eltingville.

If you have thoughts on where those neighborhood lines should actually be drawn — and plenty of people do — feel free to reach out at Mike@bkcb6.org. Lots of different opinions on this and always happy to hear them.

Like everything in the city, there's a lot going on here. SI CB3 ranks 10th citywide for median income. Staten Island has the second-highest median income of any borough, but that average covers a real divide between the South Shore and the lower-income North Shore.

District Overview — Furman Center
Demographics

In 2023, Tottenville/Great Kills was the city’s 13th largest neighborhood by population out of 59 neighborhoods. Among the 59 districts, it has the 59th largest proportion of nonwhite population, the 10th highest median income, and the 24th most expensive rents. From 2010 to 2024, the district added 26 new housing units, 26 units of which were market rate and 0 units of which were income restricted.
