District Overview — Furman Center
Demographics

MN CB7 ranks 3rd in population among NYC’s 59 community board districts. Among the 59 districts, it has the 53rd largest proportion of nonwhite population, the 6th highest median income, and the 6th most expensive rents. From 2010 to 2024, the district added 6,131 new housing units, 5,174 units of which were market rate and 957 units of which were income restricted.
