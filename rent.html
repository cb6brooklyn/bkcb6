Rental Rankings & Housing

“People looking for cheap apartments.” That is what the great guitarist Vernon Reid said New York sounded like in 1990, and if anything that line hits even harder now.

This page puts that reality in context for Community Board 6, Brooklyn, and the city. It tracks rent rankings for CB6 and all 59 community districts, but it also includes active Housing Connect listings for affordable housing and other housing resources. Because we are not here just to drown and describe the water. We are trying to help people navigate it, and do something about it.

CB6 is one of the most expensive community districts in Brooklyn, but the bigger point is that this is not just a CB6 problem. It is a citywide one.
