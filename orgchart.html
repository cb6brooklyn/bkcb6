First Deputy Mayor — Dean Fuleihan

▼

Advises the Mayor on citywide administrative, operational, and policy matters. Oversees the Office of Management and Budget, Department of Finance, Department of City Planning, Landmarks Preservation Commission, NYCHA, HPD, and related agencies.

OMB — Office of Management & Budget
nyc.gov/omb ↗ · Prepares the city's annual budget and tracks spending across all agencies. CB6 submits annual budget priorities to OMB.
DOF — Dept of Finance
nyc.gov/finance ↗ · Collects taxes, assesses property values, and processes payments. Property tax assessments affect every lot in CB6.
DCP — Dept of City Planning
nyc.gov/planning ↗ · Develops land use policy, manages zoning, and runs ULURP. Central to how neighborhoods grow and change.
LPC — Landmarks Preservation Commission
nyc.gov/lpc ↗ · Designates and regulates historic landmarks and districts. CB6 has six historic districts and reviews CoA applications.
NYCHA — NYC Housing Authority
nyc.gov/nycha ↗ · NYC Housing Authority. CB6 includes Gowanus Houses and Wyckoff Gardens. ~$200M in capital investment secured through the 2021 Gowanus rezoning.
HPD — Dept of Housing Preservation & Development
nyc.gov/hpd ↗ · Finances and regulates affordable housing citywide.
SBS — Dept of Small Business Services
nyc.gov/sbs ↗ · Supports small businesses with permits, workforce development, and commercial corridor programs.
DCWP — Dept of Consumer & Worker Protection
nyc.gov/dcwp ↗ · Enforces consumer protection and labor standards laws, licenses businesses, and investigates complaints.
DCLA — Dept of Cultural Affairs
nyc.gov/dcla ↗ · Funds and supports NYC's museums, theaters, and cultural institutions.
OPT — Office to Protect Tenants
nyc.gov ↗ · Coordinates city efforts to prevent tenant harassment, displacement, and illegal evictions.
MOME — Office of Media & Entertainment
nyc.gov/mome ↗ · Supports NYC's media and entertainment industries and issues film production permits.
NYC Tourism
nycgo.com ↗ · Promotes NYC as a travel destination and manages convention business.
PDC — Public Design Commission
nyc.gov/pdc ↗ · Reviews proposed works of art, architecture, and landscape on city-owned property.
OEO — Office for Economic Opportunity
nyc.gov/opportunity ↗ · Coordinates anti-poverty programs and measures socioeconomic outcomes across agencies.
M/WBE Office
nyc.gov/mwbe ↗ · Promotes and certifies Minority and Women-Owned Business Enterprises for city contracting.
OTWD — Office of Talent & Workforce Development
nyc.gov ↗ · Coordinates workforce training and career development programs across the city.

Deputy Mayor for Housing & Planning — Leila Bozorg

▼

Oversees housing production, land use planning, and community development. Most relevant to CB6's ULURP reviews, affordable housing work, and the Gowanus rezoning implementation.

HPD — Dept of Housing Preservation & Development
nyc.gov/hpd ↗ · Finances and regulates affordable housing. Administers MIH and related programs.
HDC — Housing Development Corporation
nychdc.com ↗ · Housing Development Corporation. Provides low-cost financing for affordable housing through bonds and other tools.
DCP — Dept of City Planning
nyc.gov/planning ↗ · Manages zoning and runs ULURP. CB6 interacts with DCP on every land use application in the district.
OHR — Office of Housing Recovery
nyc.gov ↗ · Manages post-disaster housing recovery programs including Sandy rebuild initiatives.

EDC — Economic Development Corporation

SBS — Dept of Small Business Services
nyc.gov/sbs ↗ · Supports small businesses, workforce development, and neighborhood commercial corridor programs.
DCWP — Dept of Consumer & Worker Protection
nyc.gov/dcwp ↗ · Enforces consumer protection and labor standards, licenses businesses, investigates complaints.

Deputy Mayor for Health & Human Services — Helen Arteaga Landaverde

▼

Oversees the agencies that provide direct services to New Yorkers — healthcare, housing assistance, children's services, and homelessness.

HRA/DSS — Human Resources Administration
nyc.gov/hra ↗ · Human Resources Administration. Administers SNAP, Medicaid, cash assistance, and other safety net programs.
DHS — Dept of Homeless Services
nyc.gov/dhs ↗ · Operates the city's shelter system and outreach programs for people experiencing homelessness.
ACS — Administration for Children's Services
nyc.gov/acs ↗ · Protects children from abuse and neglect, manages foster care and adoption.
H+H — NYC Health + Hospitals
nychealthandhospitals.org ↗ · NYC's public hospital system — 11 hospitals serving all New Yorkers regardless of ability to pay.

Deputy Mayor for Operations — Julia Kerson

▼

Oversees the agencies that keep the city running day to day — streets, sanitation, parks, buildings, environmental protection, and infrastructure.

DOT — Dept of Transportation
nyc.gov/dot ↗ · Manages city streets, bridges, traffic signals, and bike infrastructure. Relevant to CB6's street safety work and Sammy's Law implementation.
DSNY — Dept of Sanitation
nyc.gov/dsny ↗ · Collects trash and recycling, manages composting, and clears snow.
DPR — Dept of Parks & Recreation
nyc.gov/parks ↗ · Operates NYC's 30,000+ acres of parkland including Prospect Park — within CB6's service area.
DOB — Dept of Buildings
nyc.gov/buildings ↗ · Issues permits, inspects construction, and enforces building code. CB6 tracks DOB activity via the dashboard's address lookup.
DEP — Dept of Environmental Protection
nyc.gov/dep ↗ · Manages water supply, wastewater, and the Gowanus Canal Superfund and Red Hook CSO facility.
DDC — Dept of Design & Construction
nyc.gov/ddc ↗ · Manages capital construction projects for city agencies.
SCA — School Construction Authority
nycsca.org ↗ · Plans and builds new school buildings and manages capital improvements to existing schools.
TLC — Taxi & Limousine Commission
nyc.gov/tlc ↗ · Regulates yellow cabs, green cars, and app-based rideshare services.

Deputy Mayor for Community Safety — Renita Francois

▼

Oversees and coordinates the operations of the Department of Community Safety.

NYPD — NYC Police Department
nyc.gov/nypd ↗ · NYC Police Department. CB6 is served by the 76th Precinct (Carroll Gardens/Red Hook) and the 78th Precinct (Park Slope/Gowanus).
FDNY — Fire Department of New York
nyc.gov/fdny ↗ · Fire Department of New York. Responds to fires, medical emergencies, and hazmat incidents across CB6.
DOC — Dept of Correction
nyc.gov/doc ↗ · Operates the city jail system, including Rikers Island and borough facilities.
MOCJ — Mayor's Office of Criminal Justice
nyc.gov/criminaljustice ↗ · Coordinates criminal justice policy and reform initiatives across city agencies.
