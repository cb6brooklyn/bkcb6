District Overview — Furman Center
Demographics

SI CB1 ranks 18th in population among NYC’s 59 community board districts. Among the 59 districts, it has the 33rd largest proportion of nonwhite population, the 32nd highest median income, and the 41st most expensive rents. From 2010 to 2024, the district added 1,568 new housing units, 474 units of which were market rate and 1,094 units of which were income restricted.
