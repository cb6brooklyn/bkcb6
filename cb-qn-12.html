Like everything in the city, there's a lot going on here. QN CB12 ranks 28th citywide for median income. Queens is the most economically diverse borough in the city, ranging from high-income districts to neighborhoods with significant poverty and rent burden.

This is the district page for Queens Community Board 12. The district covers Jamaica, Hollis, St. Albans, Springfield Gardens, Baisley Park, Rochdale Village, and South Jamaica.

If you have thoughts on where those neighborhood lines should actually be drawn — and plenty of people do — feel free to reach out at Mike@bkcb6.org. Lots of different opinions on this and always happy to hear them.

Hopefully this gives you a sense of what's happening in the district — whether it's yours, one you're curious about, or one you've never thought about until now. Click around and see what you find.
