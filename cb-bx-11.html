Like everything in the city, there's a lot going on here. BX CB11 ranks 43rd citywide for median income. The Bronx has the lowest median income of any borough — four of the five lowest-income community districts in the city are here.

This is the district page for Bronx Community Board 11. The district covers Allerton, Bronx Park East, Eastchester Gardens, Indian Village, Morris Park, Parkside, Pelham Gardens, Pelham Parkway, and Van Nest.

If you have thoughts on where those neighborhood lines should actually be drawn — and plenty of people do — feel free to reach out at Mike@bkcb6.org. Lots of different opinions on this and always happy to hear them.

Hopefully this gives you a sense of what's happening in the district — whether it's yours, one you're curious about, or one you've never thought about until now. Click around and see what you find.
